# Transmission and Drivetrain Components

## Transmission Options

### Manual Transmission (Getrag 6-speed)
**Type:** 6-speed manual
**Code:** GS6-53BZ

**Components:**
- Clutch assembly (single disc)
- Release bearing
- Flywheel (dual-mass)
- Shift linkage
- Shift cables

**Common Issues:**
- Dual-mass flywheel failure (rattling)
- Clutch wear
- Shift cable bushings wear
- 2nd gear syncro wear

---

### Automatic Transmission (Aisin 6-speed)
**Type:** 6-speed automatic
**Code:** TF-80SC (AF40)

**Components:**
- Torque converter
- Valve body
- Transmission control module (TCM)
- Shift solenoids

**Common Issues:**
- Mechatronic unit failure
- Transmission fluid leaks
- Harsh shifting
- Torque converter shudder

**Fluid Capacity:** ~7 quarts
**Fluid Type:** ATF Dexron VI or BMW/MINI specific

---

## Drivetrain Components

### 1. **Clutch System (Manual)**
**Type:** Hydraulic actuated

**Components:**
- Clutch master cylinder
- Clutch slave cylinder
- Hydraulic line
- Clutch disc
- Pressure plate
- Release bearing

**Common Issues:**
- Slave cylinder failure
- Master cylinder failure
- Fluid leaks

---

### 2. **Driveshafts (CV Axles)**
**Configuration:** Front-wheel drive

**Components:**
- Left driveshaft
- Right driveshaft
- Inner CV joints
- Outer CV joints
- CV boots

**Common Issues:**
- CV boot tears
- CV joint clicking (worn)
- Vibration during acceleration

---

### 3. **Differential**
**Type:** Integrated in transmission
**Ratio:** Varies by model (typically 3.65:1 or 4.11:1)

**Issues:**
- Fluid leaks at seals
- Bearing wear (rare)
