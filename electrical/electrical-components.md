# Electrical System Components

## Power Generation and Storage

### 1. **Battery**
**Type:** 12V Lead-acid AGM
**Location:** Trunk/boot area
**Typical Capacity:** 60-80 Ah

**Common Issues:**
- Premature battery death (drains)
- Sulfation from short trips
- Cold weather failure

---

### 2. **Alternator**
**Output:** 140-150A
**Voltage:** 14.4V regulated

**Function:** Charges battery, powers electrical systems

**Common Issues:**
- Bearing failure (squealing)
- Voltage regulator failure
- Brushes wear

---

### 3. **Starter Motor**
**Type:** Gear reduction starter

**Common Issues:**
- Solenoid failure
- Bendix gear wear
- Brushes wear

---

## Engine Management

### 4. **ECU (Engine Control Unit)**
**Location:** Engine bay
**Type:** Bosch or Siemens (varies)

**Function:**
- Controls fuel injection
- Manages ignition timing
- Controls turbo boost
- Monitors emissions
- Diagnostics

**Issues:**
- Water damage (rare)
- Connector corrosion
- Software issues requiring reprogramming

---

### 5. **Ignition Coils**
**Type:** Pencil coils (4, one per cylinder)
**Location:** Top of engine, in spark plug wells

**Common Issues:**
- Coil failure (misfires)
- Oil contamination from valve cover leaks
- Connector corrosion

**Symptoms:**
- Check engine light
- Misfires (P0300-P0304 codes)
- Rough idle
- Loss of power

---

### 6. **Spark Plugs**
**Type:** NGK or Bosch
**Gap:** ~0.028-0.032"
**Interval:** 96,000 km

**Issues:**
- Fouling from oil consumption
- Carbon buildup
- Incorrect gap

---

## Sensors (Key Electrical Components)

### 7. **Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor**
**Location:** Intake tube after air filter

**Function:** Measures incoming air mass

**Common Issues:**
- Contamination
- Element failure
- Symptoms: Poor fuel economy, rough idle

---

### 8. **Oxygen Sensors**
**Count:** 2-4 (pre-cat and post-cat)
**Location:** Exhaust system

**Function:** Monitor exhaust oxygen content for fuel trim

**Issues:**
- Sensor degradation over time
- Carbon fouling
- Heater circuit failure

---

### 9. **Camshaft Position Sensors**
**Count:** 2 (intake and exhaust)
**Location:** Timing cover

**Common Issues:**
- Sensor failure
- Wiring issues
- Symptoms: No start, rough running, check engine light

---

### 10. **Crankshaft Position Sensor**
**Location:** Transmission bell housing area

**Common Issues:**
- Sensor failure (no start)
- Wiring damage

---

## Lighting

### 11. **Headlights**
**Type:** Halogen or Xenon (HID)

**Issues:**
- Ballast failure (Xenon)
- Bulb failure
- Moisture in housing
- Lens clouding

---

### 12. **Tail Lights**
**Type:** LED or incandescent

**Issues:**
- LED board failure
- Water intrusion
- Bulb failure

---

## Body Control

### 13. **Body Control Module (BCM)**
**Location:** Under dashboard

**Function:**
- Controls windows, locks, wipers
- Central locking
- Interior lights

**Issues:**
- Water damage
- Software glitches
- Window regulator control issues
