# N14 Maintenance Schedule (Kilometers - South Africa)

## Critical Maintenance for N14 Reliability

### Every 8,000 km:
- Engine oil change (0W-40 synthetic)
- Oil filter replacement
- Visual inspection for leaks

### Every 16,000 km:
- Check oil consumption rate
- Inspect valve cover for leaks
- Check coolant level

### Every 48,000 km:
- **Carbon cleaning (walnut blasting)** - CRITICAL
- Air filter replacement
- PCV valve inspection
- Cabin filter replacement

### Every 96,000 km:
- Spark plugs replacement
- Ignition coils inspection
- Brake fluid flush
- Coolant flush

### Preventative Replacements:
- **Timing chain tensioner** - If 2007-2009 and not done, DO IMMEDIATELY
- **Vacuum pump** - 130,000-160,000 km
- **Water pump** - 130,000-160,000 km
- **Thermostat housing** - At first sign of leak
- **HPFP** - Budget for replacement at 130,000+ km

### Weekly Checks:
- Oil level (N14 consumes oil - normal)
- Coolant level
- Tire pressure
- Listen for death rattle on cold start
