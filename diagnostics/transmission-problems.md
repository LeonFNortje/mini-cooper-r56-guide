# Transmission Problems

## Getrag 6-Speed Manual:
- Generally reliable
- See: [Clutch Issues](clutch-issues.md) for DMF problems
- Shift linkage can wear
- Transmission fluid every 80,000-96,000 km

## Aisin 6-Speed Automatic:
- Less common in Cooper S
- Transmission service every 80,000-96,000 km
- Mechatronic unit can fail (expensive)

**For detailed information:** `../transmission/transmission-components.md`
