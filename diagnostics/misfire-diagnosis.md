# Misfire Diagnosis

## Common Causes of Misfires on N14:

### 1. Carbon Buildup (Most Common)
- **See:** [Carbon Buildup Guide](../engine/components/intake-exhaust/04-carbon-buildup-guide.md)
- Symptoms: Rough idle, hesitation, misfires
- Solution: Walnut blasting

### 2. Spark Plugs
- Replace every 96,000 km
- Check for oil fouling

### 3. Ignition Coils
- Common failure
- Replace as needed

### 4. Oil in Spark Plug Wells
- **See:** [Valve Cover Guide](../engine/components/cylinder-head/04-valve-cover.md)
- Valve cover gasket leak
- Oil fouls coils and plugs

### 5. Low Fuel Pressure
- **See:** [HPFP Guide](../engine/components/fuel-injection/01-HPFP.md)

### 6. Vacuum Leaks
- Check intake boots and hoses

**Diagnostic Steps:**
1. Read OBD codes (P0300-P0304)
2. Inspect spark plugs
3. Check for oil in wells
4. Test ignition coils
5. Check for carbon buildup (borescope)
