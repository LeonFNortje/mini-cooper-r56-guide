# Clutch & Flywheel Issues (Manual Transmission)

## Common Clutch Problems:

### Dual Mass Flywheel Failure:
- **Symptoms:** Rattling at idle in neutral, vibration
- **Common on N14** - DMF wears out
- **Cost:** R8,000-15,000 (clutch + flywheel kit)
- **Mileage:** 120,000-160,000 km typical

### Clutch Wear:
- Slipping under load
- High engagement point
- Difficulty shifting

### Recommendations:
- Replace clutch and flywheel together
- Consider solid flywheel conversion (performance builds)
- OEM parts recommended for daily driving
