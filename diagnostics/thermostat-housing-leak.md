# Thermostat Housing Crack Diagnosis

**This information is covered in:** [Overheating Diagnosis](overheating.md)

## Quick Summary:

The thermostat housing is a very common failure point on N14 engines.

- **Material:** Plastic (prone to cracking from heat)
- **Location:** Left side of engine, near exhaust
- **Cost:** R1,500-3,500 (SA)
- **Symptoms:**
  - Coolant leak left side
  - Overheating
  - Low coolant warning
  - Sweet smell

**For complete diagnosis and repair information, see:** [Overheating Diagnosis](overheating.md)

- Thermostat housing inspection
- Coolant leak diagnosis
- Water pump issues
