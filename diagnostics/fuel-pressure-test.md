# Fuel Pressure Testing

## Testing N14 Fuel Pressure:

### High Pressure Fuel System:
**See:** [HPFP Guide](../engine/components/fuel-injection/01-HPFP.md)

**Normal Pressure:**
- Key ON: ~5 bar
- Idle: 80-100 bar
- 3,000 RPM: 120-150 bar
- Full throttle: 150-200 bar

### Testing Methods:
1. **OBD2 Scanner** - Read fuel pressure from live data
2. **Mechanical Gauge** - Most accurate
3. **Check for codes** - P0087 (low pressure)

### Low Pressure Diagnosis:
- HPFP failure (most common)
- Low pressure pump in tank
- Fuel filter clogged

**Complete testing procedures:** [HPFP Diagnosis](HPFP-diagnosis.md)
